WELCOME TO NARSINGDI PILOT HIGH SCHOOL

Message from Head Teacher's


I am delighted to know that the 12th Convocation Ceremony of Baghata High School will be held on 06 March 2017. Convocation of a school is a magnanimous and memorable occasion for the graduating students who have earned the by dint of their perseverance and devotion towards studies. the passing-out students, parents, teachers and authority of the school. Education is light and it should be based on modern knowledge, and the present government has been attaching the highest priority to modern and quality education and human resources development. I am very positive that Baghata High School is trying to provide ICT-based quality education to prepare the students for the 21st century.

Baghata High School is the first and oldest school in the country and has a long tradition and reputation of spreading the light of education. Throughout the country the school has produced thousands of qualified citizens to serve the nation. The school has been fostering its tradition for more than 182 years. Mr Mejba Uddin from his personal effort and enthusiasm established this school in 1978 and named it “Baghata High School” to spread English language all over the country. The activities of the school began on the verenda of a dochala room made of tile near Borokuthi on the bank of the river Padma. At the beginning it was a private and full free school. At that time there was no allocation of government fund for the school. So, the school continued with the assistance and lawyers, businessmen, the rajas and zamidars of Puthia, and others living in Narsingdi. But the school received’s recommendation in 1982 after a visit to the school. The school was nationalized on 20 June 1985 after the recommendation had been sent to the government.
