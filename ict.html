ICT > E-Learning




In its continual effort to “go for IT”, I.H.S has introduced e-learning for grades 1 to 8 in the subjects of English and Maths. The enormous educational value of e-learning lies in its interactive, activity-based, audio-visual format that appeals to the different learning styles of each individual child. Both the content and methodology of e-learning will help to develop the creative and critical thinking skills of learners. Specially designed software, customized to the I.H.S syllabus, will further sharpen e-skills and develop a tech-savvy generation. Students are exposed to a whole new learning experience with interactive Smart boards and online testing in place. Online Assessment to students. Online tests with Question Bank.

Computer Laboratories


The School has four Computer Laboratories, out of which, Lab 1 is equipped with 40 Pentium machines and connected through broadband internet. Labs 2, 3 and 4 are equipped with 104 IBM compatible computers connected with Novell Network. The Labs are equipped with eight printers. Students of Grade 1 to 12 are taught Computer Science as a specific subject.

Wi-Fi Campus

IHS has a holistic Wi-Fi campus, equipped for student’s use and teachers, in their classrooms. The school is equipped with smart boards and computer systems to be used while in class. More than 225 classrooms fitted with 3M Interactive Board with Multimedia Projectors. All classrooms provided with High end Fibre Optic backbone cabling and CAT6 Networking cabling for faster bandwidth.

MiStudio


We are living in an information age, where students need to be abreast with the happenings around the world with a tap of their finger. ‘Mi Studio’ is a unique concept in IHS, which empowers students to become independent, research based learners of tomorrow. The studio is equipped with latest edition iPads for collaborative work. It is used by the students to produce and present curriculum based presentations, based on their research. The ‘i’ in ‘Mi’ stands for several aspects of technology linked learning, such as interaction, inspiration, ideas, information etc.

Awards & Scholarship


Scholarship and Annual Merit Awards Scholarships and prizes are awarded every year to those students who secure the highest total marks among all the divisions of each grade. The Indian High School gold medals are presented to those students who top the school in Grade 10 and 12 CBSE examinations and silver medals are awarded to individual subject toppers at the Annual Awards Ceremony. Scholarships in cash are also awarded to the students excelling in academic and co-curricular activities in the school. The Chairman's Welfare Fund is a special fund created by the school to support the deserving and needy students by offering them financial aid in order to continue their studies.
Student Leadership


Prefectural Board The Head Boy, Head Girl, House Leaders, Deputy Leaders, Prefects, Sports Secretary and Cultural Secretary are selected from among the students to provide leadership and assume responsibility for the groups, placed under their charge. They form the Students Council, which meets periodically interacts with the school authorities on behalf of the student community and conducts various activities.
